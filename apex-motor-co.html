Streamlining test drive bookings during high-traffic weekends

Challenge

The dealership faced scheduling chaos during peak hours, with double bookings and missed test drives frustrating both customers and sales staff.

Solution

An AI voice assistant was implemented to manage inquiries, confirm bookings, and sync test drive schedules with staff calendars automatically.

Results

Weekend efficiency improved 2.5× and missed appointments dropped by 60% within the first 45 days of rollout.
